# Netflix Data Exploration & Visualization

## 📌 Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset to uncover trends, audience preferences, genre distribution, regional content patterns, and business insights.

The analysis focuses on helping Netflix understand:
- What type of content performs well
- Regional content preferences
- Growth trends in Movies vs TV Shows
- Audience targeting through ratings
- Content duration strategies

## 📊 Key Insights
- Netflix content addition increased significantly after 2015.
- Movies dominate the platform, while TV Shows have steadily grown in recent years.
- Drama, Comedy, and International content are the most popular genres.
- The United States, India, and the UK contribute the highest amount of content.
- Most content targets mature and teenage audiences (TV-MA, TV-14).
- Netflix follows a localized content strategy across different countries.

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Invest more in region-specific content.
- Expand Kids & Family content categories.
- Continue producing short-format, high-engagement content.
- Promote successful regional content globally.
